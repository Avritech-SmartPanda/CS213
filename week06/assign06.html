Who We Are

Avriteq State University is an organisation that provides education, training and development programmes to various sectors of the South African economy. Avriteq State University’ have a combined knowledge of over 30 years’ experience in Software development, Customer Services, Procurement, Human Resources, Industrial Relations, Training and Development.

What We do

We offer high intensity, quality management training, facilitation and development of courseware materials for the emerging and existing labour force of our country. We provide ICT hands-on training programs that generate, collate, synthesize and disseminate knowledge and relevant information to build a sustainable human and Enterprise capacity aligning Information technology to business in order to strengthen the quality of Organizations operation.
